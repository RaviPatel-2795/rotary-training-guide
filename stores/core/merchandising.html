Visual Merchandising Guide

Rotary Watches

Always wear watch gloves when merchandising

Merchandise products in families where possible

Ensure that family plaques are placed alongside the family

Ensure that displays are clean & dust free

Ensure you have up-to-date backboard designs

Neatly align watches & price tickets

Place watches so that the face is positioned at an optimal angle

Cover Lifetime Guarantee & Waterproof plaques with the Rotary logo plaques

Keep matching pairs together within their family

Dress gents higher than ladies

Clean dials & polish bracelets

Use horizontal c-clips to add variation in positioning

Run through your Visual Planning Guide (VPG)
